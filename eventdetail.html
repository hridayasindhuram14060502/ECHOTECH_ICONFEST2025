VR for Education Live

Discover how Virtual Reality is transforming the classroom — from immersive historical tours to interactive science simulations.

About This Event

In this free interactive webinar, educators and tech experts will explore how VR is reshaping modern education. You'll discover how virtual reality empowers students to grasp complex topics, engage in hands-on learning, and foster inclusive classrooms where everyone can thrive. The session features live demonstrations, Q&A segments, and practical guidance for educators looking to integrate VR seamlessly into their teaching.

Through immersive virtual environments, students can explore historical landmarks, conduct simulated science experiments, or even walk through mathematical visualizations — all from within the classroom. These dynamic experiences improve retention, support varied learning styles, and allow abstract subjects to come alive in ways traditional materials often can’t.

Whether you're a teacher, school administrator, or simply curious about educational innovation, this session will provide valuable insights and real-world examples. Learn how to get started with VR, access curated tools and resources, and gain inspiration for applying immersive technology in your daily lessons. Take the first step toward transforming your classroom into an engaging, future-ready learning space.

Date: June 22, 2025
Time: 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM (GMT+7)
Location: Online via Zoom
Category: Learn

What You'll Learn

  • How VR enhances student engagement
  • Best tools to create VR learning content
  • Real classroom case studies & results
  • Access to lesson plans & VR resources

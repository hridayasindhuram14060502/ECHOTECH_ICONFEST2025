What makes EchoTech VR devices eco-friendly?

Our devices are made from recycled materials and use energy-efficient components to reduce power consumption without sacrificing performance.

Do I need a PC or console to use EchoTech VR?

No. EchoTech VR works as a standalone device, making it portable and easy to use—no extra hardware required.
